## Business Insights Summary

1. Top 2 lab branches contribute over 60% of total revenue
2. Blood tests show the highest abnormal percentage
3. Repeat patients place 2x more orders than first-time patients
4. Certain orders show payment shortfalls
5. Technician productivity is uneven across branches

## Recommendations
- Improve billing follow-ups for pending payments
- Optimize technician allocation
- Focus marketing on high-value tests
